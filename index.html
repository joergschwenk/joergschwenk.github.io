About the Book

Jörg Schwenk: Guide to Internet Cryptography - Security Protocols and Real-World Attack Implications

A comprehensive textbook on Internet cryptography covering standards, protocols, and real-world attacks, intended for students, researchers, and practitioners.

This website provides companion material for the book “Guide to Internet Cryptography”. It includes downloadable figures for use in course material, solutions to selected problems, and additional resources. Existing content will be updated and extended over time, including new chapters and supplementary material.
